You send a voice note. 45 minutes later, a rider shows up at your workshop with exactly the part you need. But what happens in between?

Here's the full breakdown of how PartsDey delivery works, step by step.

## Step 1: You Place Your Order

It starts with a message. You can send a WhatsApp voice note describing what you need, or use our web app to search and select parts from the catalogue.

Either way, we need three things:
1. **What part** you need (name, OEM code, or just describe it)
2. **What vehicle** it's for (make, model, year, spec)
3. **Where to deliver** (your workshop address)

If anything is unclear, we'll ask — no guessing, no assumptions.

## Step 2: A Runner Gets to Work

Our runners are already at Ladipo or ASPAMDA market. The moment your order is confirmed and paid, the nearest available runner gets a notification.

They know the market inside out. They know which vendors stock Toyota parts, who has the best prices on German car components, and where to find those hard-to-source items.

## Step 3: Quality Verification

This is where we're different from sending your apprentice. Every part gets verified:

- The runner inspects the part physically
- They take a **QC photo** and upload it
- They compare it against your description and any photos you sent
- They record the actual vendor price

If something doesn't match, we contact you before proceeding. No surprises.

## Step 4: Dispatch

Once the parts are verified and packaged, the runner heads to the market gate pickup point. A dispatch rider is already on the way.

The handoff is fast — parts are transferred, the rider confirms the order, and they're off to your location.

## Step 5: Live Tracking

From the moment the rider picks up, you can track them in real time:

- **Live map** showing rider location
- **Dynamic ETA** based on current traffic
- **WhatsApp updates** at each milestone
- **Call rider** button if you need to give directions

## Step 6: Delivery

The rider arrives at your workshop. For prepaid orders, they hand over the parts and you're done. For COD orders, you pay the rider and they confirm receipt.

That's it. The whole process, from order to delivery, in under 45 minutes for locations within our express zone.

## What If Something Goes Wrong?

Things don't always go perfectly. Here's how we handle common issues:

- **Part unavailable:** We notify you immediately and offer alternatives
- **Wrong part delivered:** Free replacement, dispatched immediately
- **Late delivery:** We refund the delivery fee
- **Price discrepancy:** Our admin team reviews and contacts you before proceeding

We're building a service you can rely on, every single time.
